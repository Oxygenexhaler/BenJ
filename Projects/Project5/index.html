Whereabout that trash/h1>

Whereabout that trash, is an educational game for the phone dedicated to inform the player about recycling trash as well as what happens to recycled trash. As the game is a prototype it does not explore what happens to all types of trash, but focuses on teaching the player the basics of trash sorting in Denmark and the resulting processing of food trash. The different parts of the game both include a written description as well as an interactive gameplay rendition.
The first part of the game consists of the player dragging and dropping garbage in the corresponding container. This is done with a time limit, and the player can try to get as much garbage sorted as possible, earning up to three stars depending on results. Earning up to three stars it possible for any of the games.
